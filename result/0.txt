Lab Name: Nanomechanics Laboratory
Brief Intro: The Nanomechanics Laboratory at the University of Michigan's Department of Mechanical Engineering focuses on research in the field of single molecule biophysics. The lab members conduct studies and experiments to understand the mechanical properties and behavior of biological molecules at the nanoscale. Their research findings are published in scientific publications, and they welcome collaborations and inquiries from interested individuals or organizations.
