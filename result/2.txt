Lab Name: Smart Materials and Structures
Brief Intro: The Smart Materials and Structures lab at the University of Michigan, in collaboration with General Motors, aims to exploit the emerging capabilities of smart materials and structures to create innovative advanced device technologies for automotive applications. The lab conducts theoretical and experimental research to expand the knowledge base of smart materials, develops design methodologies and tools to support smart material technologies, and utilizes emerging smart materials to invent leap-frog device technologies. The lab fosters a collaborative environment between researchers and engineers to improve the progression from basic materials research to innovative technology development and its inclusion in successful, marketable products.
